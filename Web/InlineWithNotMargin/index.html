Having white space after inline block elements causes there to be a space after the element. Additionally, there is also a problem with some space being added under the element. This is because caused by the vertical alignment.

One way to have the elments line up next to each other is to remove the whitespace.

Another way is to add comments

The best way is to probably just add -4 pixels to the right margin.

And you can get rid of the bottom spacing by specifying setting vertical-align to top.
